  • What are the system requirements?
    This website requires a modern browser to work (such as Firefox or Safari). A high speed connection and fast computer are not required, but make the user experience more delightful.
  • The website doesn't work.
    Ignoring the fact that you didn't ask a question, are you sure your computer is on? If your computer is on and it still doesn't work, try turning it off and back on again.
